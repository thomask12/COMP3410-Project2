Gallery of Available Animals...

This gallery showcases animals who have been at Dumb Friends League and Denver Animal Shelter for an extended period of time (over 1 month) or are older animals (over 6 years). You can find the animals on their respective shelter websites by selecting the images below.

Animals like those above are not often adopted because of their old age or temperment. With your help, we can find all these animals a loving family.
