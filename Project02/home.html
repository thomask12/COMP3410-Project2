What is the mission of Pet Stop?

Pet Stop is dedicated to finding the perfect home for animals currently held in animal shelters in the Denver area. The perfect pet may be hard to find, but with Pet Stop you can "test drive" pets in your home before you adopt! Knowing how a new pet will behave in your home is an important part of picking out an animal that is the best match for you. For just a small fee, any animal* can spend a day with you so that you can decide for sure if the match is right for you. *Some animals cannot be brought home for visits due to aggression towards humans or other animals

Available Pets

A few older animals are showcased on the Gallery as they get adopted less often than younger animals. Althought you may want a puppy or kitten, please consider adopting older dogs and cats as they are just as affectionate (and house trained). Available animals are updated periodically. For the most up-to-date information about adoption, use the displays on the right side of the gallery page. You can also view our featured animals by moving your mouse over the images on the right of this page.
